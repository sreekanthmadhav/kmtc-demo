About the Provider

Kabani Tech Pvt Ltd supports early-stage and development-stage medical device projects by bridging the gap between clinical ideas and engineering implementation. The team can support requirement understanding, product architecture, embedded electronics, prototype development, testing preparation and coordination with regulatory or ISO 13485 pathways.

Core Service Capabilities

Medical Device Concept Engineering Translation of clinical problem statements into technical requirements and development plans.
Electronics & Embedded Systems PCB architecture, sensor integration, firmware, communication modules and device control logic.
Prototype Development Functional prototype development, iterative testing, enclosure coordination and hardware validation.
Documentation Support Engineering notes, test plans, risk inputs and development evidence for regulatory pathway preparation.
UI/UX and Digital Interface Mobile apps, dashboards, device interfaces and visualization systems for medical technology products.
Manufacturing Transition Support Prototype-to-production support, vendor coordination and technical handover preparation.

Supported Development Stages

Clinical Need Requirement capture, user workflow mapping and technical feasibility discussion.
Concept System block diagram, architecture planning, component strategy and proof-of-concept planning.
Prototype Electronics, firmware, enclosure coordination, pilot build and functional verification.
Validation Test setup support, data collection, design iteration and validation-readiness documentation.
Regulatory Pathway Technical inputs for ISO 13485 consultants, CDSCO consultants and manufacturing partners.
